Final is a access modifier
Finally is a block in exception handling
Finalize is the method of object class.

Final: It is a access modifier applicable for classes, methods and variables.
Final classes cannot be inherited, final methods cannot be overridden and final variables cannot be modified. Final variables are also known as constants.

Finally: It is a block in exception handling which will be executed always irrespective of whether exception is raised or not and whether exception is handled or not.

Finally block is executed after try and catch only.  No matter you handle it or not. I will be executed.

Finalize: Used to perform clean up processing just before object is garbage collected. It is a method of object class. It is called by garbage collector just before destroying object to perform clean up processing.
Finalize method is called by garbage collector before destroying object. It is used to perform clean up processing just before destroying object.
